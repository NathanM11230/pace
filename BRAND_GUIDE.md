# 🏃 Pace - Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Pace

**Tagline Options:**
- "Run at your Pace" (primary)
- "Stay on Pace with what matters"
- "Information at your Pace"
- "Your Pace. Your Feed."

**Positioning Statement:**
Pace delivers personalized audio snippets that keep you informed while you run, commute, or move—bite-sized content on your schedule, at your pace.

---

## Brand Voice

**Tone Attributes:**
- Energetic but not hyper
- Informed but not academic
- Friendly but not cutesy
- Motivating but not preachy

**We sound like:** A smart running buddy who always has something interesting to share

**We don't sound like:** A news anchor, a fitness coach, or a tech bro

**Example messaging:**
- ✅ "Your morning run just got more interesting"
- ✅ "60 seconds. One story. No commitment."
- ❌ "Optimize your information consumption during cardio sessions"
- ❌ "Get pumped with knowledge, bro!"

---

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

**Primary:**
- Electric Blue (#0066FF) - Energy, movement, trust
- Or: Vibrant Orange (#FF6B35) - Energy, enthusiasm, warmth

**Secondary:**
- Deep Navy (#1A1F36) - Professionalism, depth
- White (#FFFFFF) - Clean, minimal

**Accent:**
- Bright Green (#00D9A3) - Progress, achievement
- Light Gray (#F5F7FA) - Backgrounds, subtle elements

**Recommendation:** Go with **Electric Blue** as primary. Orange is trendy but might feel dated quickly. Blue has longevity (see: Stripe, Linear, Twitter).

### Typography

**Display/Logo:**
- SF Pro Rounded (Apple) - Modern, friendly, tech
- Or: Inter - Clean, geometric, scalable

**Body/UI:**
- System fonts for mobile (SF Pro on iOS, Roboto on Android)

**Tone:** Sans-serif only. No serifs. This is modern, fast, digital.

### Logo Concepts

**Option 1: Wordmark**
```
PACE
```
Simple, bold, all caps. Clean type treatment.

**Option 2: Icon + Wordmark**
```
[>] PACE
```
Play button suggests audio, forward motion. Simple geometric shape.

**Option 3: Waveform**
```
/‾\_ PACE
```
Audio waveform integrated with text. Shows audio-first nature.

**Recommendation:** Start with **Option 1** (wordmark only). Add icon later if needed. Simplest is strongest.

- Snippet completion rate (did they listen all the way?)
